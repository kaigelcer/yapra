Instructions

In YAPRA, the dashboard view is your "stickies" view. Each sticky on the dashboard corresponds to a category of your life and contains associated tasks and notes. For example, right now my categories looks like this:

  • Coding Projects
  • Job Search
  • Music
  • Errands
  • Health / Fitness
  • Other

But yours could look totally different; when I was in school I had a category for every course. The idea is to divide up your life in a way that is logical to you.

Get Started

Press the big green button to create a new category and a corresponding sticky note

You can now:

  • Edit the category's name and color
  • Add and edit tasks
  • Write down relevant notes

Once you've created some categories and added some tasks, proceed to the calendar. Whatever tasks you add to the stickies on your dashboard will be reflected on the calendar.
