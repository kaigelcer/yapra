Why Another Productivity App?

Since high school, I have maintained an agenda using the Stickies app on my computer; I divide my life into distinct categories, each getting its own Sticky. I have a unique workflow and notation for keeping track of tasks, events, and other info on these Stickies. Organizing and viewing my life this way helps me to prioritize my tasks as well as analyze which aspects of my life are demanding more of my time (and which parts are being neglected).

But my method has limitations. Stickies are just plaintext. I have no way of syncing events to my calendar, getting notifications, etc. So what's the solution? Well, there are many productivity apps which come very close to emulating my personal workflow described above. But none of them match my workflow exactly — and realistically none ever will. So I'm making my own and tailoring it exactly to my liking. But hopefully others will enjoy its simplicity ¯\_(ツ)_/¯.
